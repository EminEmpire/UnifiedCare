about us

Transforming holistic care to enable people who need support to have independent and happy lives

Unified Care Ltd is an independent care provider that aims to deliver care and support to people in a holistic way.

Each of our services provides a home from home environment, with the added security of highly trained and dedicated staff. We support our residents to live as independently as they can and to achieve their goals via our person centred approach.

Our service users each have their own room, which they can decorate and furnish to suit their own taste, which we believe helps to make it truly a home. We actively encourage our service users to participate in a wide range of activities and for them to engage as much as possible with the wider community.

Why choose Unified Care ?

Unified Care is dedicated to unity, working together to support our service users with heart-driven care. We involve everyone closely connected to our service users in discussions about their care and treatment to achieve positive outcomes.

Read more For each of our service users, we establish achievable goals that foster growth and transition. We emphasize teaching and encouraging independence in a safe and structured environment. Our support extends to helping service users achieve their goals, ranging from basic cooking sessions to gaining qualifications and paid employment through attending college.

If it is important to our service users, it is important to us.

- Unified Care

At Unified Care, we take a holistic approach to our service users’ care, and feel that focusing not just on their specific care needs, but also on their wider lives and interests, can only have a positive effect on them as a whole.
We want our service users to not only feel part of the Unified family, but also part of the wider community. As such, we actively encourage them to continue with, or indeed start, activities they may have enjoyed before coming to one of our homes
